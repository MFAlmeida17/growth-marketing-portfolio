### Post 1: Old SEO vs. New SEO Shift (12 Ways SEO is Expanding)

* **Source Link:** [LinkedIn - Jake Ward]([https://www.linkedin.com/in/jakezward/](https://www.linkedin.com/in/jakezward/))

* **Date Collected:** June 16, 2026

* **Core Framework:** "SEO is changing. But NOT in the way you think. Because the fundamentals are still 90% the same. It's not a full reset, but a recalibration."

| Metric / Dimension | Old SEO Approach | New SEO Framework |

| :--- | :--- | :--- |

| **Focus** | Rank pages (Win positions on Google) | Influence answers (Be referenced across platforms) |

| **Strategy** | Keyword targeting (Match search queries) | Entity positioning (Own topics and become the source) |

| **Asset** | Content pages (Blog posts and service pages) | Knowledge assets (Original data, expert POVs, frameworks) |

| **Journey** | Linear funnel (TOFU -> MOFU -> BOFU) | Darker search (5+ invisible touchpoints before search) |

| **Keywords** | Text-based keyword queries (2-3 words, search-volume first) | Conversational + multimodal (Questions, images, files, video, context) |

| **Content** | Skyscraper articles (3,000+ word guides) | Expert-backed answers (Concise, named experts, first-person) |

| **Publish** | Publish often (More content, more traffic) | Publish less, distribute more (One idea everywhere it matters) |

| **Links** | Build backlinks (Acquire links at scale) | Earn citations (Get referenced by trusted sources) |

| **SERP** | Featured snippets (Win prime real estate) | AI Overviews + agents (Be the cited source) |

| **Metric** | Rankings + traffic (Clicks from search) | Visibility + citations (Mentions and branded search uplift) |

| **Outcome** | Win the click (Drive traffic to site) | Win the selection (Be picked by humans and agents) |

| **Mindset** | Chase "new" tactics (Shiny acronyms and tactics) | Just do SEO + marketing well (90% SEO basics + 10% extra focus) |

### Post 2: SEO in 2026 (The Playbook for AI Visibility)

* **Source Link:** [LinkedIn - Jake Ward]([https://www.linkedin.com/in/jakezward/](https://www.linkedin.com/in/jakezward/))

* **Date Collected:** June 16, 2026

* **Core Framework:** Good SEO now means achieving maximum AI visibility. Search volume as a whole is up 26% since ChatGPT launched.

#### 1. SEO (Search Engine Optimization)

* **What it means:** Standard search optimization.

* **The Playbook:** Target keywords, build backlinks, refine on-page content, and secure strong technical foundations.

* **The Data:** Still remains the foundational bedrock of organic traffic.

#### 2. AEO (Answer Engine Optimization)

* **What it means:** Optimizing to become the direct answer inside engines.

* **The Playbook:** Implement FAQ and How-To schema, deliver direct answers up top, and format content with "AI-friendly" structures.

* **The Data:** 90% of AEO is just good SEO done well. Manual tweaks to `llms.txt` or `.md` files have 0 measurable impact if fundamental authority is missing.

#### 3. GEO (Generative Engine Optimization)

* **What it means:** Optimizing for generative AI engines (Claude, ChatGPT, Perplexity).

* **The Playbook:** Build high Author Authority (E-E-A-T), earn long-tail semantic citations, get mentioned on niche authority platforms, and ensure the brand shows up in competitor comparisons.

* **The Data:** Every major LLM either retrieves live index from search or was trained on data weighted by the same authority signals Google uses. 

#### 4. AIO (AI Optimization)

* **What it means:** Broadly optimizing for visibility within downstream AI models and automated agents.

* **The Playbook:** Drive multi-platform cross-presence, maintain entity consistency across the web, nurture brand knowledge graph signals, and earn earned media coverage.

* **The Data:** Optimizing for AI comes down to getting mentioned on authoritative sources (so models learn you exist) and ranking well in traditional search (so they cite you live).
